#### overview of major types of threats ######

1) Malware : generic term form software that has alicious purpose.(virus,worms,adware,trojans)

2) security breaches : group of attacks that attempt to gain unautorised access to your system.(privilage excalaltion,breaking into servers,cracking passswords)

3) DoS attacks : attacks to prevent legitimate access to your system.(DDoS)

4) web attacks : attack to attempt to breach your website.(SQL injection,cross site scripiting)

5) session hijacking : attempts to take over a session.

6) Insider threats : (the most important one)

7) DNS poisoing : compromises dns servers so that users can be redireccted to malicious website.
